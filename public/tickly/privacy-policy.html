Privacy Policy - Tickly

Last updated: December 28, 2025

Tickly, a product of GeoSoft ("we", "our", or "us"), is a todo and task management app designed with your privacy in mind.

Tickly does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your tasks, todos, and app data are stored locally on your device only.

No Data Collection

We do not collect any information from you. Tickly operates entirely offline and locally on your device. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, and no analytics.

Local Storage Only

All your tasks, lists, and preferences are stored exclusively on your device. We have no access to this data, and it never leaves your device.

Data Deletion

When you uninstall Tickly, all app data is automatically removed from your device. There is nothing stored on our servers to delete because we never collect anything in the first place.

No Third-Party Services

Tickly does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your usage of the app remains completely private.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@tickly.com
