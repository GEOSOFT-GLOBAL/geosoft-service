Privacy Policy - ng-tax

Last updated: March 12, 2026

ng-tax, a product of GeoSoft ("we", "our", or "us"), is a free Nigerian tax calculator app designed with your privacy in mind. It helps you calculate various taxes based on the latest Nigerian tax laws without any form of authentication.

ng-tax does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All tax calculations and app data are processed locally on your device only.

No Data Collection

We do not collect any information from you. ng-tax operates entirely offline and locally on your device. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, and no analytics.

Local Processing Only

All tax calculations, settings, and preferences are processed and stored exclusively on your device. We have no access to this data, and it never leaves your device.

Data Deletion

When you uninstall ng-tax, all app data is automatically removed from your device. There is nothing stored on our servers to delete because we never collect anything in the first place.

No Third-Party Services

ng-tax does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your usage of the app remains completely private.

Tax Calculator Features

ng-tax provides the following features, all of which operate locally on your device:

  • Personal Income Tax (PIT) calculations
  • Value Added Tax (VAT) calculations
  • Company Income Tax (CIT) calculations
  • Withholding Tax (WHT) calculations
  • Capital Gains Tax (CGT) calculations
  • Stamp Duty calculations
  • Tax rate references based on current Nigerian tax laws
