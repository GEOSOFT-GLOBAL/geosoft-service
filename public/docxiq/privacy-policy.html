Privacy Policy - DocxIQ

Last updated: December 28, 2025

DocxIQ, a product of GeoSoft ("we", "our", or "us"), provides AI-powered document analysis services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email, name, and profile details when you register.
  • Documents: Files you upload for analysis (processed securely and not stored permanently).
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our services, including AI credits usage.
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, and device identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered document analysis
  • To track and manage your API quota and credits
  • To improve our AI models and services
  • To provide customer support

Document Processing

Documents uploaded to DocxIQ are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers unless you explicitly save them to your account.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your documents and personal information.
