Privacy Policy - Quant

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Quant, a product of GeoSoft ("we", "our", or "us"), is a free mobile calculator app designed with your privacy in mind. It offers basic and scientific calculator functionality without any form of authentication.

Quant does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All calculations and app data are processed locally on your device only.

No Data Collection

We do not collect any information from you. Quant operates entirely offline and locally on your device. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, and no analytics.

Local Processing Only

All calculations, settings, and preferences are processed and stored exclusively on your device. We have no access to this data, and it never leaves your device.

Data Deletion

When you uninstall Quant, all app data is automatically removed from your device. There is nothing stored on our servers to delete because we never collect anything in the first place.

No Third-Party Services

Quant does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your usage of the app remains completely private.

Calculator Features

Quant provides the following features, all of which operate locally on your device:

  • Basic calculator operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division)
  • Scientific calculator with advanced functions
  • Natural notebook display for complex equations
  • Unit converters for various measurements
  • Mathematical constants and formulas
