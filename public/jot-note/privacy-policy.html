Privacy Policy - Jot-Note

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Jot-Note, a product of GeoSoft ("we", "our", or "us"), is a free note-taking app designed with your privacy in mind. It allows you to create, edit, and delete notes without any form of authentication.

Jot-Note does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your notes and app data are stored locally on your device only.

No Data Collection

We do not collect any information from you. Jot-Note operates entirely offline and locally on your device. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, and no analytics.

Local Storage Only

All your notes, settings, and preferences are stored exclusively on your device. We have no access to this data, and it never leaves your device.

Data Deletion

When you uninstall Jot-Note, all app data is automatically removed from your device. There is nothing stored on our servers to delete because we never collect anything in the first place.

No Third-Party Services

Jot-Note does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your usage of the app remains completely private.

Note-Taking Features

Jot-Note provides the following features, all of which operate locally on your device:

  • Create new notes with text content
  • Edit existing notes
  • Delete notes you no longer need
  • Organize and manage your notes locally

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@jot-note.app
