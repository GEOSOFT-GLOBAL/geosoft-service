Privacy Policy - LinkShyft

Last updated: December 28, 2025

LinkShyft, a product of GeoSoft ("we", "our", or "us"), provides link management and URL shortening services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email, name, and profile details when you register.
  • Link Data: URLs you shorten and associated metadata.
  • Analytics Data: Click statistics, geographic data, and referrer information for your links.
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, and device identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide URL shortening and link management services
  • To generate analytics and insights about your links
  • To detect and prevent abuse of our services
  • To improve our services and user experience

Link Analytics

We collect anonymized data about clicks on your shortened links to provide you with analytics. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify visitors.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your link data and personal information.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@linkshyft.com
